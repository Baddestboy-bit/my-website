About Us

Nateete Preparatory School, established in 1995, has been a cornerstone of quality education in the heart of Nateete. For over three decades, we have been dedicted to nurturing young minds and shaping future Leaders through a holistic to education.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing and inclusive enviroment where every child can achieve their full potential academically, socially, and emotionally

Our Vision

To be leading institution that inspires excellence, fosters innovation, and prepares students to thrive in a rapidly changing world.
